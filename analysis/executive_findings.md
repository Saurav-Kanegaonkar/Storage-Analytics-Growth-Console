# Executive Findings

## What I analyzed

I combined market-level channel performance, tracking QA, available inventory, competitor pricing, and experiment backlog data.

## Findings

- The dataset contains 12,000 channel-day rows, 2,400 inventory records, 1,600 competitor price checks, and 18 experiment candidates.
- The strongest experiment candidate is EXP007 (Checkout simplification) with a priority score of 73.9.
- The most test-ready market is M014 with 4.52% booking conversion and 294.2 average available units.

## Recommendation

Prioritize experiments where demand, measurement quality, and operational capacity overlap. Do not scale paid traffic into markets where inventory capacity cannot absorb successful demand.
