# Executive Findings

## What I analyzed

I combined synthetic market-level channel performance, tracking QA, available storage capacity, competitor pricing, and experiment backlog data.

## Findings

- The strongest immediate experiment is the mobile unit selector because it has high impact, high tracking readiness, and enough capacity fit to make a positive result operationally useful.
- Competitor price guardrails should support paid media budget reviews, but they are not the main growth test.
- Online move-in proof messaging is a strong second product test because it targets reservation-to-move-in abandonment.
- Competitor price data is the weakest operational feed and needs freshness checks before any automated budget rule is trusted.

## Recommendation

Prioritize experiments where demand, measurement quality, and operational capacity overlap. Do not scale paid traffic into markets where storage capacity or competitor pressure would make successful demand hard to serve.
